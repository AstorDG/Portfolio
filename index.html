Introduction

I'm a Northeatern graduate with a BS in computer science and game development. I was inspired to become a software developer from the wonder I had while playing video games growing up. I wanted to know how these worlds came to life. The iterative process of building software from concept to completion provides me with immense satisfaciton and purpose. I love watching what I build come to life by seeing people interact with it. Whether they're enjoying a game I've made. Or using a tool I made that solves a problem they have freeing them up to work on more imporant and complex problems. My best work is done in the context of working with others. Being in an environment where multiple perspectives can come together enhances the problem solving process and expands my creativity. I see myself and other come up with ideas we may never have thought of without a different perspective. The end result is made better by containing the best of us.
